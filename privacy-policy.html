We value your privacy and want to be transparent about how we handle your data. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect your information. Information We Collect This Chrome extension requires access to your Google account in order to upload screen recordings you create to your Google Drive. Specifically, the extension collects: Google Drive Access: We request access only to upload files to your Google Drive through the https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.file scope. This grants us permission only to manage files created by the extension and not other files in your Drive. Recorded Video Files: The extension processes video files that you record with the screen recording feature. How We Use Your Information Uploading to Google Drive: The only purpose for accessing your Google Drive is to upload screen recordings that you initiate. We do not store, track, or share any personal data or recordings. Your Google Drive authentication is handled through Google's OAuth 2.0 protocol, ensuring secure and encrypted authentication. How We Protect Your Information All interactions with your Google account are secure, using OAuth 2.0 standards. We do not store or share any authentication tokens or information.
